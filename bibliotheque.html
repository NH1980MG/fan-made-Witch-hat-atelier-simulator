Central sigils

Nine central sigils define the recipe material or governing action. Decorative sigils remain editable but do not enlarge the validated matrix.

Modifier signs

Thirty-eight signs modify supply, state, form, motion, target, scope, relation, or power. Size, position, connection, and rotation affect the result.

Supports
